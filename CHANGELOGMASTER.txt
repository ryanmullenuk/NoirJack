Noir Market V1.3 Changelog

Version alignment
- Updated app title, manifest version, service worker cache and save target to V1.3.
- Retained loading support for V1.2 and older save keys, then migrates active saves to noir_market_v1_3.

UI
- Added a scrolling news ticker at the top of the main screen.
- Added Rank, Days Held and Reputation to the main Status panel.
- Added Hustle as a new main action.

Dynamic economy and trading
- Added city-specific market prices, supply and trend tracking.
- Added daily price movement across all cities.
- Added player-action market impact: large sales depress that item price in the current city for 1-3 days.
- Prices recover gradually as the temporary crash expires.
- Added elite supplier benefit when reputation reaches 81+.

Market rumours
- Added one daily rumour.
- Rumours are generated as 60% accurate and 40% false.
- Rumours cover shortages, police crackdowns and price collapses.

News ticker and market events
- Added daily news events which directly affect prices.
- Added event types covering police crackdowns, festival demand, rodents destroying stash houses and customs seizures.

Reputation system
- Added reputation score from 0-100.
- Successful trades increase reputation.
- Loan repayments increase reputation.
- Avoiding arrest for a day increases reputation.
- Arrests, missed loan payments and debt default behaviour reduce reputation.
- Low reputation increases robbery risk.
- Higher reputation improves lender limits.
- Elite supplier access unlocks at 81+ reputation.

Crime and risk
- Added arrest checks during day advancement and travel-related outcomes.
- Minor offences apply fines between £500 and £5,000.
- Major offences apply jail time between 1 and 7 days, with time advancing automatically.
- Added police bribe option with base 40% success chance, improved by reputation.
- Added random mugging events when carrying more than £20,000 cash, with 5-25% cash loss.

Hustle
- Added Informant feature under the new Hustle section.
- Informants cost £1,000-£10,000.
- Informants have an 80% chance of accurate information and a 20% chance of scam outcome.

Storage and vaults
- Preserved city-specific vaults.
- Added city-specific vault upgrade levels:
  - Basic: 100 slots, free
  - Secure: 250 slots, £100,000
  - Fortress: 500 slots, £500,000
- Vault value now contributes to net worth.

Ranking
- Added rank progression based on net worth.
- Net worth includes cash, bank balance, inventory value, vault value and debt.
- Player must remain above a rank threshold for 5 consecutive days to earn that rank.
- If net worth drops below the current rank threshold, rank drops immediately.

Preserved existing functionality
- Splash screen logo retained.
- Dust particle effects retained.
- Background music retained and explicitly looped through the audio element, with synth fallback.
- Shady lenders retained.
- City vault system retained.
- Flight seizure warnings retained.

NOIR MARKET V1.7 CHANGELOG

Release focus: Travel Shipping System

Added
- Travel section now has two modes: TRAVEL and SHIPPING.
- TRAVEL keeps the existing flight-based city movement and airport warning flow.
- SHIPPING allows drugs to be exported to another city before the player travels.
- Exported drugs are removed from carried inventory and stored as a pending shipment for the destination city.
- IMPORT allows pending shipments to be collected once the player reaches the destination city.
- Imported shipments are added into the destination city vault storage.
- Shipping cost is calculated as 5% of the shipment value plus a £200 fee.
- Shipments track origin city, destination city, item quantity, value, cost and day sent.
- V1.7 save structure now includes a shipments array.
- V1.6 and earlier save migration retained.

Preserved
- Existing flight travel.
- Airport seizure warnings.
- Weapon and drug seizure mechanics when travelling by air.
- City-specific vault storage.

- Dynamic economy, rumours, news ticker and reputation systems.
- Proportional loan interest from V1.5.
- Home screen icons from V1.6.
- Splash screen logo, dust particles and background music.

Notes
- Shipping does not advance the day by itself.
- The player must still travel to the destination city to import shipped stock.
- Shipments require enough vault capacity at the destination city before they can be imported.
